Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID)

  • Crash data

This information is collected only by Google, not by us. Google follows its own Privacy & Safety policies: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Since we do not collect personal data, the only data usage comes from Google AdMob:

We do not store, access, or share this data.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our game does not collect personal data from children or any users.
